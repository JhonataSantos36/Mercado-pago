Mandatory information to receive payments

When making a payment, we need you to send us the following information from your server:

  • Amount to be paid.
  • Payer email: just in case something unusual happens during or after the payment process. It's a protection for the payer, that improves the post-payment service.
  • Payment method: card brand or cash payments entity (ATM or over the counter). Notice that every payment method has its particularities, for example: for card payments, the card number and security code length, and for other payment methods (offline), there is an accreditation time.
  • Issuer (just for cards): the card issuer. We arrange interest-free installments with them in order to provide the best experience for your users.
  • Installments (just for cards): the amount of installments selected by the user. We know if the payment should be processed with or without interests depending on the payment method and the card issuer.
  • Token (just for cards): We need your user's card number, cardholder name, expiry date and security code. In some countries, we'll need you to get an identification type and number. MercadoPago creates a tokenized version of this data in order to protect the card's sensible information.

Sandbox and productive mode

With the personalized Checkout you can receive sandbox or productive payments. The difference between one and the other radicates in the credentials' type you'll use to authenticate to our API.
Develop your app using your sandbox mode credentials. When it's ready to receive productive payments, you'll have to go through an homologation process and replace the credentials for the productive ones. No code changes needed. This way, you can make test payments while your users are making productive payments, by just using the corresponding credentials.
Sandbox mode is an exact replica of production mode, design to make it easier to test your app during integration. The usage of sandbox mode does not generate any real charges. Beside setting the mentioned credentials, in sandbox mode you should use the provided test cards.

Use your credentials

Two types of keys:

  • Public key: identifies your MercadoPago account. Not secret. Used in the frontend.
  • Private key (or access token): allows access to account private operations. These keys must be confidentially kept in your backend servers and never be published.
These keys have to usage modes:
  • Sandbox mode: allows test payments.
  • Production mode: allows real payments.
With the public key, you can access to the following information through our SDK:
  • Payment methods (payment_methods)
  • Banks (card_issuers)
  • Installments (installments)
  • Identification types (identification_types)
  • Data tokenization (card_tokens)
Besides, with the private key you can access to the following resources:
  • Payments
  • Refunds
  • Customers
  • Cards
